About

Who we are

Save Earth Save Life is dedicated to enhancing Cambodia's ecological health through comprehensive environmental services. Their mission includes conducting meticulous Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA), biodiversity surveys, and promoting renewable energy initiatives. They aim to lead in environmental protection, biodiversity conservation, and sustainable practices, envisioning a future where economic growth aligns harmoniously with ecological integrity. By prioritizing renewable energy solutions, they strive to reduce carbon footprints and foster a cleaner, greener Cambodia.

Our Mission

Our mission is to contribute significantly to the ecological well-being of Cambodia by providing high-quality environmental services. We aim to protect and enhance the natural environment through meticulous Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA), biodiversity and ecology surveys, renewable energy projects, and sustainability initiatives. By combining our expertise and resources, we strive to ensure that development in Cambodia is both environmentally responsible and sustainable.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leader in environmental services and renewable energy in Cambodia, setting the standard for excellence in environmental protection, biodiversity conservation, and sustainable practices. We envision a future where economic growth is harmoniously balanced with ecological integrity, and where renewable energy plays a key role in reducing carbon footprints and promoting a cleaner, greener Cambodia.

Services

What we do offer

Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA)

Conducting thorough assessments to identify, predict, and mitigate the environmental impacts of development projects, ensuring compliance with both national and international regulations.

Biodiversity Surveys

Performing detailed studies of local flora and fauna to understand and preserve the region's rich biodiversity, including habitat assessments and conservation planning for endangered species and ecosystems.

Ecology Surveys

Performing thorough ecological research to examine interactions within ecosystems, monitor their health, and propose efficient management approaches.

Renewable Energy Projects

Developing and implementing renewable energy solutions, such as solar, wind, and hydroelectric power, to reduce carbon emissions and promote cleaner energy alternatives.
